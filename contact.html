Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to emails?

We typically respond to all emails within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4-6 hours.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support primarily through email. This allows us to provide detailed, written responses and maintain records of all communications.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing your ideas and suggestions. Send them to feedback@speaktomaps.com and we'll consider them for future updates.

Do you have a bug reporting system?

Yes! If you encounter any bugs or issues, please email us at support@speaktomaps.com with details about what happened and what device/browser you were using.

Other Ways to Connect

While email is our primary communication method, you can also:

  • Visit our website: https://boldbull.github.io/speakmaps/
  • Check our FAQ: Look for answers to common questions on our main page
  • Report issues: Use the support email for any technical problems

Response Times

Here's what you can expect when contacting us:

  • General inquiries: 24 hours
  • Technical support: 4-6 hours
  • Bug reports: 2-4 hours
  • Feature requests: 48 hours
