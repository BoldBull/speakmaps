About Speak to Maps

Our Mission

At Speak to Maps, we believe that navigation should be as simple as speaking. Our mission is to make getting directions faster, easier, and more accessible for everyone. We've created a tool that bridges the gap between voice commands and Google Maps, eliminating the need to type addresses or download additional apps.

What We Do

Speak to Maps is a revolutionary web-based application that converts your speech directly into Google Maps navigation. Simply say any address out loud, and we'll instantly open it in Google Maps on your device. Whether you're driving, walking, or planning a trip, our service makes navigation effortless.

Why We Built This

We noticed that while voice assistants are becoming more common, there wasn't a simple, direct way to convert speech to Google Maps navigation. Users had to either type addresses manually or use complex voice commands through various apps. We wanted to create something that was:

  • Simple: Just speak and go - no complex setup or learning curve
  • Fast: Instant results without waiting for app downloads or account creation
  • Private: No data collection, no tracking, no accounts required
  • Accessible: Works for everyone, including those with mobility or vision challenges

Our Technology

Speak to Maps leverages modern web technologies to provide a seamless experience:

  • Web Speech API: For accurate voice recognition on supported browsers
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for all screen sizes and devices
  • Progressive Web App: Fast loading and offline-capable
  • Privacy-First Architecture: All processing happens locally on your device
